OUR SOUND

Formed in the crucible of Seattle's underground scene, Rocky Thunder fuse the raw power of '90s grunge with the intricate melody of modern alternative rock. Our music is an exploration of dissonance and harmony, a soundtrack to the blurred lines between chaos and calm.

Since our debut EP, we've focused on creating a live experience as electrifying as our studio work. We're currently touring regionally and prepping for our biggest national tour yet. Follow us on social media for show dates and exclusive behind-the-scenes content!
